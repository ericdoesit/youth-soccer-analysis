The Real Math Behind Club Soccer

The scale of youth soccer and the variety of options available for athletes is staggering. Making sense of the information is difficult even for participants. We want to change that.

I'm building a public database of youth soccer program costs — what clubs actually charge by region, level, and age group. The survey and site are both a work in progress.
